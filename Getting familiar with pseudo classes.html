How to make a paper airplane

  • First take an A4 size paper
  • *any color of your choice*
  • Place in a horizontal way and fold the
    two upper corner into half.
  • Now fold it backwards into half
  • Finally place the paper on any side and
    again fold it into half, same with the other side.
